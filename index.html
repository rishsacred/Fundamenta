Who We Are

Fundamenta is a nonprofit organization based in Ontario dedicated to improving financial literacy among students. We believe that financial education is a fundamental life skill that should be accessible to all.

Our Vision

To create a generation of financially responsible individuals who can make informed decisions about their money and future.
